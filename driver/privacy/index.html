Privacy Policy

What Driver needs from you
  • Your Google account
What Drive gets from you
  • Your Google Drive account
  • Your name
  • Your email address

If you import your own Drive Folder:

  • Folder images
  • Image metadata
Does Driver share this information?

No.

Where are these collected information stored?

These information is stored on-device.

Can Driver modify something?

No. Driver only has READ access to your Google Drive folder.

Why Driver needs these information.

Driver only needs these information for essential (like using the Drive API which requires access to your Google Account) and cosmetic purposes (like your name and email in the app's settings)

I have questions. Where can I contact you?

That's good to hear! feel free to contact me via e-mail and on my Discord server.
